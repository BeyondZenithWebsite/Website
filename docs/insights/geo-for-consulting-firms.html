Quick FAQ

Is GEO different from SEO?

Yes. SEO helps ranking in search results; GEO helps retrieval and citation inside AI-generated answers.

What should we implement first?

FAQ blocks, schema, stronger internal linking, and pages that answer high-intent buyer questions directly.
