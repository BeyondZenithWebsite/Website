CTO delivery scorecard template (free)

By BeyondZenith • Updated 27 Feb 2026 • 4 min read

Use this template weekly to detect delivery drift early and drive action. Keep it simple: 5 metrics, 5 owners, 5 decisions.

Metric 1: Cycle time

Median days from started to done.

Metric 2: Predictability

Planned vs delivered ratio per sprint/week.

Metric 3: Quality

Escaped defects + rework rate.

How to use it

Who should own this?

Engineering lead owns the scorecard. Product + leadership review weekly decisions from it.

How often should we update?

Weekly, same day and time, with explicit actions assigned.
