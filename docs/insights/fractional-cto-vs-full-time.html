Fractional CTO vs full-time CTO

If you need senior technical decisions now but not a permanent C-level seat yet, fractional leadership usually wins on speed and cost.

Fractional CTO

Lower fixed cost, fast onboarding, and immediate decision velocity.

Full-time CTO

Best for long-horizon org design, executive hiring, and permanent leadership presence.

Decision trigger

Choose fractional when outcomes are urgent and headcount flexibility matters.
