Operating Model

How we work: 30–90 day engagement model

A compact execution model for teams that need senior technical decisions and measurable momentum quickly.

1) Assess (Week 1–2)

Map delivery drag, architecture risk, and decision bottlenecks. Baseline the KPIs we’ll move.

2) Align (Week 2–3)

Re-sequence priorities, define ownership, and lock weekly decision cadence with leadership.

3) Execute (Week 3–8)

Implement high-leverage technical and operational interventions with clear rollback paths.

4) Transfer (Week 8–12)

Document working model, train internal owners, and hand over sustainable operating rhythm.

What gets measured

Cycle time, release predictability, escaped defects, incident rework, and decision latency.

Typical outcomes

Higher delivery confidence, faster execution, fewer fire drills, and clearer leadership visibility.
