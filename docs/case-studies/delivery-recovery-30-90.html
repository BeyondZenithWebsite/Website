Logistics quoting workflow

Baseline: Quote turnaround averaged ~2 business days with frequent reconciliation errors.

Intervention: Workflow redesign, automation checkpoints, owner-based exception handling.

Outcome: Quote turnaround reduced to under 4 hours; pricing/reconciliation errors reduced by 35%.

Healthcare platform delivery

Baseline: Delivery unpredictability and unstable release cadence across teams.

Intervention: Delivery governance reset, dependency sequencing, weekly decision cadence.

Outcome: Release predictability improved from ~58% to ~84% within 8 weeks.

Cloud product team acceleration

Baseline: Incident-driven rework and high-priority deadlines repeatedly slipping.

Intervention: Architecture guardrails, WIP limits, and execution-focused leadership rhythm.

Outcome: Incident rework reduced by 32%; release cycle time improved by 29% in 6 weeks.
