Representative outcomes

Logistics quoting workflow

Baseline: Quote turnaround averaged ~2 business days with frequent reconciliation errors.

Intervention: Workflow redesign, automation checkpoints, owner-based exception handling.

Outcome: Quote turnaround reduced to under 4 hours; reconciliation errors down 35%.

Healthcare platform delivery

Baseline: Delivery predictability was unstable across platform and product streams.

Intervention: Governance reset, dependency sequencing, weekly leadership cadence.

Outcome: Release predictability improved from ~58% to ~84% within 8 weeks.

Cloud product acceleration

Baseline: Incident-driven rework and repeated slippage on high-priority releases.

Intervention: Architecture guardrails, WIP limits, execution-focused operating rhythm.

Outcome: Incident rework down 32%; release cycle time improved 29% in 6 weeks.

Detailed case: logistics SaaS delivery recovery

Context: Scale-up logistics SaaS, 22-engineer team, strong demand but weak delivery confidence.

Problem: Hidden cross-team dependencies, late integration failures, and frequent priority churn.

Diagnosis: High replan frequency, unclear ownership of dependency risk, and low visibility on architecture constraints.

Intervention: Weekly delivery governance, roadmap resequencing by risk/impact, and architecture simplification on two high-friction service boundaries.

Outcome: Within 45 days, planning confidence improved and releases moved from quarterly batching toward biweekly increments on priority streams.
