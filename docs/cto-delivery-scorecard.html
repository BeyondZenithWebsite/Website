CTO Delivery Scorecard

Self-assessment for delivery predictability, engineering velocity, leadership structure, architecture complexity, and AI adoption readiness.

How scoring works

Score each dimension from 1–5. 1 indicates high risk and low reliability. 5 indicates consistent operating maturity and strong predictability.

Assessment dimensions

Delivery predictability

How often does the team ship against realistic commitments?

Engineering velocity

Are cycle time and throughput improving without quality degradation?

Technical leadership structure

Are technical decisions timely, owned, and aligned with business priorities?

Architecture complexity

Is architecture enabling delivery or creating dependency drag?

AI adoption readiness

Can AI initiatives move from pilot to production with measurable outcomes?

Scoring interpretation

5–10: urgent intervention likely required. 11–17: targeted recovery plan needed. 18–22: solid foundation with specific gaps. 23–25: strong operating maturity.
